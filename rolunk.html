About Us

Welcome to the DeluxeNetwork Minecraft server! We are the best place to take your Minecraft world to new levels. DeluxeNetwork started in 2020 when a handful of avid Minecraft fans decided to create a community where everyone could have fun and find servers that best fit their playstyle.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: to provide the best Minecraft experience for our players. Everyone can find their place here, whether it's building, survival, PvP or even minigames. Our servers are constantly developing and expanding to always offer new and exciting challenges.

Our community

The DeluxeNetwork community is one of our most important resources. We are proud to have a friendly and supportive community. We regularly organize community events, tournaments and other programs where players can meet, play together and make new friends.

Our staff

DeluxeNetwork is backed by a dedicated and experienced team who work tirelessly to ensure that the server always runs smoothly and that players get the best possible experience. Our moderators and administrators are always ready to help and ensure that the community rules are followed.
